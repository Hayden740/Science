# Earth in Space — Mission Passcodes (TEACHER ONLY)

Students need these to unlock "The Science" section in each mission,
after they have written their CER claim.

| Mission | Passcode |
|---|---|
| 1 — Why Don't We Fall Into Space? | `gravity` |
| 2 — Why Does the Sun Seem to Move? | `rotation` |
| 3 — Why Do We Have Seasons? | `seasons` |
| 4 — Why Does the Moon Change Shape? | `moon` |
| 5 — What Causes the Tides? | `tides` |
| Final — Is Earth Really Special? | `earth` |

Passcodes are not case-sensitive. Note: this repository is public, so treat
the passcodes as a pacing tool rather than a secure lock.
